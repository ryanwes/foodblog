Beef Roast

Beef roast is an great comfort recipe. It is also great if you have errands or busy work to be done around the house you can just throw the ingredients in the crock pot set and go.

  • 3 - 3 1/2 lb boneless beef chuck roast
  • 1 Tbsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 shallots, chopped
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 2 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • 3 medium sweet poatoes cut into 2 inch chunks
  • 3 rosemary sprigs
  • 3 thyme springs
  1. Season roast with salt, onion, and garlic powder
  2. Heat olive oil over medium high heat in a large frying pan. Sear the roast on each side for about 2 minutes per side until well browned. Place the roast in slow cooker.
  3. In the same pan add onions and shallots and cook on medium heat until soft
  4. Add beef stock, wine, and worcestershire sauce to onioin and shallot mixture and stir well. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, scraping all the brown bits from the pan. Pour the onion broth mixture over roast in slow cooker.
  5. Add sweet potatoes around the roast. Add rosemary and thyme sprigs
  6. Cover and cook on high for 5 hours or on low for 8 to 10 hours. Slice or shred roast and serve with sweet potatoes and onion sauce from the slow cooker
