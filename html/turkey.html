Turkey Burger

Sometimes there is nothing I want more than a burger. This burger recipe is delicious but is also a healthy alternative to the popular beef option. The lean ground turkey paired with fresh onion and spinach creates a perfect marriage of health and flavor.

  • 1 lb of turkey (lean, ground)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp spicy brown mustard
  • minced onion (as much or as little as you want
  • chopped arugula/spinach (as much or as little as you want)
  1. Combine and form into patties.
  2. Cook on stovetop until cooked through.
  3. Top with cheese until melted, remove from the stove.
  4. Serve on whole wheat bun with spicy brown mustard, sliced tomato, and sliced avocado.
